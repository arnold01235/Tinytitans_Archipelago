# Elias — Super Mario 64

## What you need

- A **Super Mario 64 ROM** (USA, unmodified) — you need to source this yourself
- The **Archipelago client** installed: [archipelago.gg/downloads](https://archipelago.gg/downloads)

## Step 1 — Patch your ROM

1. Open the Archipelago launcher
2. Click **"Open Patch"** and select the `.apsm64` patch file you receive from the host after the session is generated
3. This will produce a patched `.z64` ROM — keep it somewhere easy to find

## Step 2 — Launch the SM64 client

1. In the Archipelago launcher, open **Super Mario 64 Client**
2. It will ask you to locate your patched ROM
3. The client will start an emulator automatically (or you can use your own N64 emulator and connect separately)

## Step 3 — Connect to the server

1. In the SM64 client, enter the server address in the top bar (e.g. `archipelago.gg:12345`)
2. Enter your **slot name** (Elias) and the room password if there is one
3. Click **Connect** — you should see a confirmation message

## Step 4 — Play

- Stars are your checks. Collecting a star sends an item to someone in the multiworld.
- You may receive items that give you extra lives, moves, or other stars.
- Check the client chat for messages when items are sent or received.

## Tips

- Make sure the emulator and client are both running at the same time
- If you disconnect, just reconnect from the client — your progress is saved server-side
