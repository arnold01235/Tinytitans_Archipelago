# Hans — Zelda: Ocarina of Time

## What you need

- An **Ocarina of Time ROM** (USA 1.0) — you need to source this yourself
- The **Archipelago client** installed: [archipelago.gg/downloads](https://archipelago.gg/downloads)
- The OoT Archipelago setup is handled entirely through the Archipelago launcher — no separate randomizer needed

## Step 1 — Patch your ROM

1. After the host generates the session, you will receive a `.apz5` patch file
2. Open the Archipelago launcher and click **Open Patch**
3. Select your `.apz5` file and point it to your OoT ROM
4. A patched ROM will be created — save it somewhere easy to find

## Step 2 — Launch the OoT client

1. In the Archipelago launcher, open the **OoT Client**
2. It will ask you to select your patched ROM and will launch an emulator automatically
   - You can also use your own emulator (e.g. RetroArch, Bizhawk) and connect the client separately

## Step 3 — Connect to the server

1. In the OoT client, enter the server address (e.g. `archipelago.gg:12345`)
2. Enter your slot name (`Hans`) and password if needed
3. Click **Connect** — you should see a confirmation in the client log

## Step 4 — Play

- Chests, item pickups, and dungeon rewards are your checks
- You may receive items from other players' worlds (hearts, songs, equipment, etc.)
- The client chat shows you what was sent and received

## Tips

- Make sure to use the patched ROM, not your original ROM
- If items stop syncing, close and reopen the client while keeping the emulator running
- Dungeon order may be different from the original — check the OoT map for accessible areas
